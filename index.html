Engineering & Enterprise

Built to
Perform.

Nomad Racers is a student engineering team from Haileybury Almaty, competing in the F1 STEM Racing Competition. Our goal: engineer a vehicle achieving the optimal balance between aerodynamic drag reduction and structural integrity.

We utilize Digital-Twin simulations to ensure peak performance — mirroring the real Formula One development process: CFD simulation first, precision CAD modelling second, CNC CAM manufacturing third.

We operate like a professional outfit — full enterprise, branding, and budget management functions running in parallel to our engineering work. Strategic lead Dariya manages partnerships while the engineering team pushes performance limits.

Beyond racing, we mentor new teams, run community workshops across Almaty, and share our technical knowledge. Teams we have mentored have gone from zero to competition-ready.
